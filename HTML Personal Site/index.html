About

Fatima Aleshaiker is a multi-disciplinary artist with an MA from Goldsmith's University of London. Her most recent projects include exhibiting From the Window an illustrated series that explores the immigrant experience, tattooing at Yalla Punk festival in Philadelphia and designing costumes for Pick of the Vine, a series of short plays and PINS, an interactive burlesque experience. Fatima fuses her background in cultural theory and education with intuitive creativity, folk traditions and visual elements drawn from her story and heritage to develop a unique body of work.
